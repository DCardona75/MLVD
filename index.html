EVENTS

Join us for an upcoming event at Iglesia la voz de Dios inc! We believe that life is better together, and our events are a great opportunity to connect with others and grow in your faith. Our events are designed to be welcoming and inclusive, so whether you're new to Church or have been a long-time member, there's something for everyone. From worship services and Bible studies to community outreach events and social gatherings, we have a variety of events to help you feel connected and engaged.

At Iglesia la voz de Dios, we believe that Church is not just a place to go on Sundays, but a way of life that transforms us and the world around us. Our events are a chance to experience this transformational power of faith in action, as we come together to worship, learn, serve, and have fun.So come join us! Check out our calendar of events on our website and RSVP to let us know you're coming. We can't wait to see you and journey together in faith.
